High-probability Q&A bridge (after 10:00)

Use this only if judges ask follow-up questions. This section is outside the 10-minute clock.

Q: Why compare against heuristic baseline, not only random?

A: Heuristic reflects real operator behavior better than random. If uplift beats heuristic, the claim is practical, not artificial.

Q: Why are capture and robust different?

A: Capture is central estimate in nominal scenario. Robust is conservative performance under uncertainty scenarios.

Q: Why can mean and weighted mean differ?

A: Weighted mean emphasizes high-risk zones from photo-informed prior; plain mean treats all walkable cells equally.

Q: Is this map real or synthetic?

A: Real context photo is for interpretation; computation uses transparent synthetic geometry for reproducibility.

Q: How big can this business become?

A: Starting SAM is ~15k high-output farms. At 1% penetration that is ~150 sites and ~£1.35m ARR at current pricing.

Q: Why this pricing?

A: Pilot price de-risks adoption; subscription price is tied to ongoing measurable decision value and audit readiness.

Q: What do you need right now?

A: Two introductions, one trial site, and one support letter for a £5k-£20k LINCAM PoC application.

Q: How is funding used?

A: Pilot evidence generation, reliability improvements, and conversion-ready reporting pipeline; all milestone-linked.
