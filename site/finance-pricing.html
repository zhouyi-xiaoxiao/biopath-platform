Pilot: £5k / 8 weeks Subscription: £9k / site / year Pilot -> Subscription: 35% Year-1 Cost Baseline: £28k Year-end target: 8 paid sites

Why this price

Buyer behavior alignment

  • Early buyers prefer a short paid pilot to reduce adoption risk.
  • The pilot price is low enough for practical approval, high enough to validate willingness to pay.
  • Annual subscription reflects ongoing decision value and reproducible reporting value.

Value justification

  • BioPath does not only suggest points; it produces benchmark-backed evidence.
  • This reduces rework and avoids ad-hoc placement cycles.
  • The price is tied to measurable operational outcomes, not feature count.
