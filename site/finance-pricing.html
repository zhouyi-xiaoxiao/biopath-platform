Why this price is credible

Buyer-behavior fit

  • Pilot-first reduces adoption risk and accelerates first decision.
  • Annual subscription reflects ongoing optimization and audit needs.
  • Price is tied to decision quality and evidence reliability, not feature count.

Service scope that supports renewal

  • Versioned site configuration updates as layouts change.
  • Scheduled re-optimization + benchmark reruns.
  • Audit-ready evidence packs (coordinates + rationale + metadata).
