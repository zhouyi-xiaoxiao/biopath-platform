Originality is robust optimization under uncertainty, not a static rule list.

Core method

  • BFS reachability features and bottleneck structure.
  • Stochastic movement modelling.

Why it matters

  • Decisions remain stable across movement scenarios.
  • Every run is reproducible and auditable.
  • Evidence quality is sufficient for pilot funding conversations.
