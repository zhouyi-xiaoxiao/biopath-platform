Slide 3 · Problem

Manual trap placement is hard to standardise and easy to underperform.

Current pain

  • Blind spots in complex layouts.
  • Inconsistent outcomes across operators.
  • No reproducible optimisation logic.

Business consequence

  • Higher labour for repeated re-positioning.
  • Uncertain capture performance.
  • Difficult auditability for IPM workflows.
